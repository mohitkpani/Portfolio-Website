Project Overview

During my internship at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, I worked on the "Analysis of Industrial Machinery" project, which aimed to evaluate the performance and efficiency of machinery used in aerospace manufacturing. I was responsible for collecting and monitoring comprehensive operational data from various machines, focusing on critical parameters such as speed, torque, and power consumption. This data collection was crucial for understanding the machinery's operational conditions and identifying areas for improvement.

In addition to data collection, I conducted a thorough failure analysis to pinpoint recurring breakdown issues and proposed preventive maintenance schedules to enhance machine reliability. I also analyzed energy consumption patterns to identify inefficiencies and recommended adjustments to optimize performance and reduce waste. This project not only deepened my knowledge of industrial automation and mechanical systems but also provided practical experience in developing effective maintenance strategies and optimizing machinery efficiency.
